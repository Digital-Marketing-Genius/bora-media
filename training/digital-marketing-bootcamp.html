Who is this for?

Designed for marketers, founders, and career-switchers who want practical, portfolio-ready skills—without weekday commitments.

What you’ll be able to do

  • Build a full-funnel strategy and channel plan

  • Ship a high-converting landing page and test plan

Curriculum (8 Weekends)

Each weekend blends strategy + hands-on labs. You’ll apply concepts to a single capstone brand throughout.

01

Marketing Strategy & Foundations

Funnel basics, ICPs, JTBD, messaging, goal setting, KPIs. Introduce naming conventions & source-of-truth docs.

02

Measurement: GA4 & GTM

Event models, conversions, UTMs. Tag manager setup, consent, QA, debugging, and Looker Studio reporting intro.

03

Paid Search (Google Ads)

Account structure, keyword strategy, match types, ad writing, extensions, bidding, PMax vs Search, audits.

04

Paid Social: Meta & LinkedIn

Objectives, audiences, creatives, remarketing, lead gen forms, budget pacing, creative iteration loops.

05

YouTube & Video

Creative specs, brand safety, sequencing, skippable/non-skippable, view-through conversions, lift testing.

06

SEO, Content & Email

Keyword research, on-page, technical basics, content calendars, lead magnets, lifecycle email journeys.

07

Landing Pages & CRO

Briefs, wireframes, offer architecture, social proof, speed, A/B testing, heuristics & analytics-driven UX.

08

Optimization, Dashboards & Careers

Routine optimization checklist, budget reallocation, Looker dashboards, portfolio polish, interview prep.
