YouTube Remarketing Campaign Launch Checklist

Launch confidently with the exact segments, exclusions, creatives, and campaign patterns we use.

Download the Checklist Scroll the checklist

1) Tracking foundations

Confirm measurement is live before building audiences.

  • Google Ads (or GA4) tag / pixel installed and firing required.

Source: tracking prerequisite in the checklist. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

2) Audience segments (build across 30/90/180 days)

SegmentTimeframeDescription
All website visitors30, 90, 180 daysAnyone that has visited your website.
Pricing / demo / trial / case-study bouncers30 daysViewed high-intent pages and left.
All YouTube video viewers30, 90, 180 daysAnyone that watched 50–100% of your videos.
All YouTube subscribers30, 90, 180 daysAnyone subscribed to your channel.

Build all cohorts for coverage and testing. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

3) Exclusions (apply account-wide)

ExclusionDescription
Age ranges < 25Exclude users under 25 if not in your ICP.
All thank-you page visitsExclude users who already submitted a form.
Existing customersExclude current customers/contacts.
Music channelsExclude YouTube music inventory.
Kids channelsExclude children’s channels.
Foreign-language channelsExclude channels outside your language.

Keeps budget focused on net-new, relevant users. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

4) Creative set (map assets to cohorts)

Asset themeTimeframeDescription
Product marketing30, 90 daysShow capabilities and outcomes.
Thought leadership90, 180 daysTeach and build authority.
Social proof30, 90 daysResults, testimonials, case studies.
Offers30, 90 daysDemo, trial, or other conversion offer.

Pair fresher cohorts with harder CTAs; older cohorts with value content. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

5) Campaign setup

  • Disable Targeting expansion to keep cohorts pure. important
  • Name patterns for clarity:
    • {Location} | {Offer} | Remarketing (30D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
    • {Location} | {Offer} | Remarketing (90D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
    • {Location} | {Offer} | Remarketing (180D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
    • {Location} | {Offer} | {Offer Name Bouncers} (30D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
  • Create all applicable remarketing campaigns before launch.

Keeps reporting tidy and aligned to intent & recency. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
