Meta Remarketing Campaign Launch Checklist

Use this field-tested checklist to launch Meta retargeting that’s cleanly measured, properly segmented, and built to scale.

Download the Checklist Or scroll the checklist
Meta Pixel Audiences Exclusions Creative Mix Campaign Setup

Before You Launch

Make sure your foundation is solid.

  • Confirm the Meta Pixel is installed and firing on all key pages/events.
  • Validate event deduplication if using CAPI + Pixel.
Tip: Verify in Events Manager, test across devices, and ensure consistent parameters.

Audiences to Build

Create remarketing segments across multiple timeframes (30 / 90 / 180 days) to match intent.

  • All website visitors
  • Past event RSVPs / attendees
  • Video viewers (25–95%)
  • Facebook page visitors
  • Lead gen forms: opens + submits
  • Pricing / demo / trial / case-study page bouncers (30D)
  • Instagram account visitors
  • Meeting no-shows (30D)
  • Closed-lost opportunities (90/180D)

Layer recency windows to modulate bids, budgets, and offers by intent depth.

Exclusions (Protect Budget)

Add exclusions to avoid wasted impressions and duplicated conversions.

  • Age ranges < 21 (or per policy)
  • Thank-you page visitors
  • Lead gen form submits
  • Existing customers & partners
  • Competitors
  • Poor-fit titles / irrelevant industries
  • Lookalike of disqualified leads

Creative & Offers by Timeframe

Map messages to buyer readiness using multiple recency windows.

  • Product Marketing (30/90D): capability highlights, feature carousels, comparison points.
  • Thought Leadership (90/180D): educational content, POVs, frameworks.
  • Social Proof (30/90D): testimonials, case stats, earned media.
  • Offers (30/90D): demo, trial, quote, pricing—aligned to funnel stage.
Tip: Build distinct ad sets per timeframe; rotate creatives to prevent fatigue and maintain recency fit.

Campaign Setup & Naming

Create separate campaigns for each timeframe and “offer bouncers.”

  • 30-Day Campaign: {Location} | {Offer} | Remarketing (30D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
  • 90-Day Campaign: {Location} | {Offer} | Remarketing (90D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
  • 180-Day Campaign: {Location} | {Offer} | Remarketing (180D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
  • Offer Bouncer (30D): {Location} | {Offer} | {Offer Name Bouncers} (30D) | {Ad Type} | {Objective}
Targeting: disable Audience Network/Expansion; focus on feed & stories placements for intent-heavy cohorts.

QA Before Publish

Final checks to keep data clean and performance predictable.

  • Campaign objectives match optimization goals.
  • UTMs standardized; pixels + events verified.
  • Frequency controls & creative rotation set.
  • Exclusions applied at the right levels.

Rollout Notes

Start with recent windows for highest intent, then expand:

  • Launch 30D + Bouncers first; add 90D/180D as performance stabilizes.
  • Use separate ad sets for timeframes to control budgets and reporting.
  • Refresh creative every 3–4 weeks for 30D cohorts; every 6–8 weeks for 90/180D.
