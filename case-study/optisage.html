The challenge

  • Visitors bounced before understanding the value proposition; free-trial conversion stuck at 1.2%.
  • Messaging emphasized features over seller outcomes and profit impact.
  • Weak CTAs and limited social proof (few logos/testimonials/case studies).
  • Poor mobile experience and slow load speeds suppressed intent.

Key insights

  • Users wanted clarity in seconds: “What will this do for my margins and Buy Box?”
  • Heatmaps showed attention clustering around pricing and outcomes—proof blocks and calculators were underutilized because they were buried.
  • Primary CTA inconsistency (“Try now” vs “Get started”) diluted action and test power.

Strategy

  • Message-market fit: outcome-first copy (“Track. Forecast. Win the Buy Box. Grow profit.”) with subhead that speaks to seller pains.
  • Single primary CTA in the hero — Start Free Trial — repeated across the page.
  • Proof architecture: testimonials, logos, and bite-size case proof adjacent to CTAs.
  • Action CTAs supporting discovery: “Calculate My Profitability” and “See My Buy Box Data.”
  • Performance: image optimization, code splitting, and priority hints to drive ≤2.0s LCP on mobile.

Execution

  • Analytics + heatmaps (GA4, Looker Studio, Hotjar) informed wireframes and copy.
  • A/B tests on hero headline, subhead, and CTA language; proof placement variations.
  • Componentized layout so new proof and feature cards can be shipped weekly without dev overhead.

Creative & experience

What changed on the page

Clear hero with one CTA, value-led feature blocks (tracking, forecasting, profitability), prominent proof, and sticky mobile CTA for faster flow to signup.
