Local Plumbing Company: 469% ROAS in 6 Months

With clean tracking, message–page match, and device-led bidding, a U.S. plumbing brand turned ad spend into measurable revenue.

Industry: Home Services Location: United States Stack: GA4 • GTM • Google Ads • Looker Studio • Hotjar

The challenge

  • No conversion tracking—media costs weren’t tied to booked jobs.
  • Poor targeting & setup led to wasted spend and missed intent.
  • Leadership skeptical digital could deliver meaningful ROI.

Key insights

  • Mobile dominated — 85%+ of spend and 88% of revenue. UX had to be tap-first.
  • Generic ad → generic page flows bled intent. Service-specific pages were vital.
  • Speed-to-contact determined win rate. Call routing & instant alerts improved close rates.

Strategy

  • Make every dollar attributable with GA4 + GTM.
  • Message–page match for each service (drains, heaters, trenchless).
  • Bid strategy aligned to high-intent conversions, not clicks.

Execution

  • Service-specific ad creatives & offers tested rapidly.
  • Mobile-first UX: sticky call button, one-screen forms, map/directions.
  • Automation: instant lead alerts, day-parting for emergencies.

What we learned

  • Mobile ~85.5% cost, ~83.6% clicks, ~87.9% conversion value.
  • Computers ~10–12% — critical for higher-ticket quotes.
  • Tablets/TV negligible (<1%).

How we optimized

  • Mobile-first UX with tap-to-call & maps.
  • Bid multipliers favoring phones; day-parting for emergencies.
  • Desktop tailored to complex jobs with longer copy & financing widgets.
