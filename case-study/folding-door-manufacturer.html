The challenge

  • Under-attributed growth: media impact on revenue was unclear, making scaling decisions risky.
  • Lead handling gaps: slow follow-ups and uneven territory coverage cost opportunities.
  • Sales friction: pricing/timeline objections stalled deals; learnings weren’t feeding ad messaging.

Key insights (6 months)

  • Capturing & addressing objections up front lifted close rates without increasing spend.
  • AI routing by location improved speed-to-contact and rep fit, raising conversion at the same CPL.
  • With attribution cleaned up, budgets moved to the highest revenue-per-sale regions and campaigns.

Strategy

  • Measurement-first plan: GA4 + GTM stitched Google/Meta to sales & revenue in HubSpot.
  • Objection-led messaging across ads, pages, and nurtures (price, timelines, quote shopping).
  • Efficiency focus: shift bids/mix toward higher revenue per sale segments.

Execution

  • HubSpot CRM rollout: automated follow-ups for cooling leads; re-engagement of older inquiries.
  • AI lead routing by location for faster contact and better rep alignment.
  • Systematic objection capture → content/FAQ updates and ad copy iterations that preempt friction.

What changed in 6 months

  • Sales attributed to Bora Media rose to 14.6%.
  • Blended CPL improved to $126 while average cost per sale fell to $1.79k.
  • Average sale (attributed) climbed to $20.8k—higher value deals at steady media spend.

Why it mattered

  • Faster speed-to-contact captured more high-intent demand before competitors.
  • Sales insights fed back into ads and pages, improving close without raising spend.
  • Leaders could scale with confidence—revenue impact was visible weekly.
