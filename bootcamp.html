Frequently asked questions

Is this bootcamp beginner friendly?

Yes. You should be comfortable using a computer and browsing the internet, but you do not need previous campaign management experience. More experienced marketers will use the projects to sharpen strategy and build stronger portfolios.

How much time should I plan for each week?

Plan for about two to three hours for the live session plus two to three hours for assignments and practice. You can go deeper if you want to extend your portfolio pieces.

Will I get access to recordings?

Yes. All live sessions are recorded and available for a set period after the cohort so that you can revisit key concepts while you complete your projects.

How do internship opportunities work?

At the end of the cohort, top performers will be invited to a short interview process with partner marketing agencies. Final internship decisions rest with each agency, but the bootcamp team will make recommendations based on project quality and participation.

Can I expense this to my employer?

Many companies invest in training for their teams. After you apply, you will receive a receipt and a short summary you can send to your manager if you want to request reimbursement.
