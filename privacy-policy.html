Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: . If you have questions, email us at hello@boramedianetwork.com.

1) Who we are & scope

Bora Media (“we,” “us,” “our”) provides performance marketing services and educational resources. This Privacy Policy applies to boramedianetwork.com and any pages where it’s posted. By using our site, you agree to the terms described here.

2) Data we collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, company) via forms, downloads, or inquiries
  • Application & enrollment info for trainings/bootcamps
  • Support messages and survey responses

Information collected automatically

  • Usage & device data (IP, pages viewed, links clicked, referrer, approximate location)
  • Cookies, pixels, and similar technologies for analytics, personalization, and ads

Information from third parties

  • Lead sources, ad platforms, analytics tools, and partners (in accordance with their policies)

3) How we use your data

  • To provide content, services, quotes, and support
  • To analyze performance and improve site experience
  • To personalize messaging and advertising
  • To send updates, offers, and educational materials (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To meet legal, security, and compliance obligations

4) Lawful bases (where applicable)

We rely on consent, performance of a contract, legitimate interests (e.g., improving services, preventing fraud), and compliance with legal obligations.

5) Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar tech for essential functionality, analytics, and advertising. You can manage preferences via your browser settings and our cookie controls below.

Do Not Sell/Share My Personal Information

6) Analytics & advertising

We may use Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and other platforms to measure performance and deliver relevant ads. These providers use cookies or mobile IDs per their policies. You can opt out via platform settings or industry tools (e.g., NAI/DAA).

7) How we share data

  • Vendors & processors (hosting, email, analytics, payment, CRM) under contracts
  • Advertising/measurement partners (aggregated or pseudonymous where possible)
  • Legal or safety reasons (court orders, preventing fraud or abuse)
  • Business transfers (merger, acquisition, asset sale)

8) Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes noted above or as required by law. We also consider contract terms and operational needs.

9) Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we can’t guarantee absolute security.

10) International transfers

Your data may be stored or processed in countries with different laws than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

11) Children’s privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We don’t knowingly collect data from children.

12) Your choices & rights

Marketing preferences

Opt out of marketing emails by using the “unsubscribe” link or by contacting us.

Cookies & ads

Use your browser settings to block cookies and visit platform ad settings to control personalization.

GDPR (EEA/UK/Swiss) rights

Access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing, and data portability. You may withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing).

California (CPRA) rights

Know, delete, correct, opt out of “sale”/“sharing,” and limit certain uses of sensitive data. Use the controls on this page or contact us to exercise rights.

Submit a Privacy Request

13) Links & third parties

Our site may link to other websites. We aren’t responsible for their content or privacy practices. Review their policies before providing information.

14) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll revise the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
