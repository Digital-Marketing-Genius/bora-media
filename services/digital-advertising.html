Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our digital advertising approach.

Which platforms do you manage?

Google Ads (Search, Shopping, PMax, YouTube), Meta (Facebook/Instagram), GDN/Display, and Bing. We’ll extend to others if the model fits.

How do you handle budgets and pacing?

Budgets ladder to monthly targets with weekly pacing. We reallocate to the highest-yield channels/ad sets as data accumulates.

Who owns the ad accounts and data?

You do. We work in your accounts and leave everything better than we found it—tracking, audiences, and creative.

How soon will we see results?

Most clients see measurable improvements within 30–60 days, with larger gains after 90 days of test-and-learn.

Do you work with our sales team?

Yes. We close the loop between ads, CRM, and revenue so optimization is based on quality and LTV—not just form fills.
