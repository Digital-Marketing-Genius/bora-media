Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our branding & design engagements.

Do we have to do a full rebrand?

No. Many clients start with a refresh—clarifying messaging, tightening visuals, and building reusable components before a full rebrand.

How long does a typical engagement take?

4–6 weeks for a refresh; 8–12 weeks for a full system with guidelines and collateral, depending on scope and feedback cadence.

What deliverables do we get?

Brand strategy doc, messaging hierarchy, visual identity, brand book, creative templates, and export packs (SVG, PNG, PDF, fonts).

Can you work with our in-house or agency teams?

Yes. We often partner with product, marketing, and sales teams to integrate the system into websites, ads, and collateral.

How do you measure success?

We align brand goals with funnel metrics: on-site conversion, lead quality, sales velocity, and paid creative performance.
